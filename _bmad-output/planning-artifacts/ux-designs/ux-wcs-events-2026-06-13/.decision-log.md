# Decision Log — wcs-events UX

_Append-only. Finalize distills this into DESIGN.md and EXPERIENCE.md._

## Session started: 2026-06-13

### Locked from PRD Section 10

- **Palette:** Near-black with warm undertone + white. No accent hue. Hierarchy via typography only.
- **Typography:** Sharp display serif (headings) + clean sans-serif (body/UI). High visual contrast between the two.
- **Hero:** Single `<h1>` "West Coast Swing in Tulsa". No subheadline, no hero image, no CTA button.
- **Layout:** Mobile-first. Tonight View must work one-handed on a phone.
- **Card hierarchy:** When → Where → Who → Cost. This sequence is intentional and locked.
- **Voice:** Confident, direct, unhurried. Welcoming to newcomers. Spare copy. Authoritative without exclusionary.
- **Aesthetic:** Edge and sophistication. Not a community center flyer, not a tech startup.
- **Navigation:** Submit link in footer only — not in primary nav.

### Direction selected: B — Blueprint

- Near-black background: `#101214` (slightly cool undertone — slight blue-grey, barely off true black)
- Color warmth: **keep B's `#101214`** — user confirmed they like it as-is. PRD "warm undertone" reconciled: the warmth lives in the white (slightly warm white, not pure `#ffffff`) rather than the black.
- Display serif: **Cinzel** (weight 400) — geometric precision, naturally Art Deco adjacent
- Body/UI sans-serif: **Josefin Sans** (weight 300) — ALL CAPS with letter-spacing 0.15–0.2em for all labels and metadata; THE Art Deco typographic move
- Navigation: **bottom tab bar** — TONIGHT · BROWSE · ARCHIVE in Josefin Sans ALL CAPS
- Browse filters: **inline collapsible** — compact bar above event list, expands vertically on tap

### Art Deco direction: CONFIRMED — Variant B "Gilded"

- Art Deco personality: subtle and unmistakable, evocative of Tulsa's oil-boom heritage
- Decorative elements: thin paired double-rules flanking hero title and section headers
- Card dividers: centered diamond ◆ ornament (not full-width rule)
- Gold accent: `#c9a84c` at 22–30% opacity on structural/geometric elements ONLY (rule lines, diamond ornaments, badge borders, active tab indicator) — NEVER on text
- **PRD "no accent hue" constraint is consciously relaxed** — gold is used only as a whisper on geometry, not as a color hierarchy tool
- Corner radius: **minimal** — `3px` on badges/chips/buttons/filter bar; `4px` on cards. Geometric without being harsh.
- Card treatment: 1px warm-white border at ~12% opacity; no fill surface
- Event type badge: small pill, white stroke, no fill, small-caps or tight sans label
- Dense but organized — more events visible per screen than Velvet/Marquee
- Personality reference: contemporary arts venue's digital schedule board

### Empty state — orientation before pitch

User feedback: when there are no events tonight, the user should immediately understand that — before encountering the subscribe copy. The locked PRD copy stays, but it is preceded by a clear "no events" signal.

Decided treatment:
- After the "TONIGHT" section label, show: "QUIET TONIGHT." in Josefin Sans ALL CAPS, ~16px, text-primary, centered — direct acknowledgment of the empty state, in the site's voice
- Below that: the locked subscribe copy and form as before
- Rationale: the subscribe copy ("Some of the best nights are planned last minute...") makes more sense once the user knows why they're seeing it. Without the orientation line, users may think the page is broken or still loading.

### Locked from Architecture

- CSS custom properties: `--color-bg`, `--color-surface`, `--color-text-primary`, `--color-text-muted`, `--font-display`, `--font-body`, `--space-xs/sm/md/lg/xl`
- Event type badge values: social-dancing, group-lesson, workshop, competition, convention
- Fit signal labels: Beginner-friendly, Partner-welcome, Skill level target, Instructor present, Special guest present
- Recurring badge text: "Recurring"
- Empty state copy (locked): "Some of the best nights in this community are planned last minute. Subscribe — you won't want to miss them."
